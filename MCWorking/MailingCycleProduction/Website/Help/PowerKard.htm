PowerKard
PowerKards are designed as a "master" to be printed in large quantities, and overprint timely information economically on this master with market update messages later.


The PDF design file of the PowerKard should contain two pages as shown below.


The messages, such as listed and sold, open houses, and other timely information specified for the schedule event will be overprinted on the second page of the PowerKard design as shown below and mailed to the farm list.


The easy to use PowerKards allow planning the whole year's mailings ahead of time.
